About

A exploration into the craft of woodworking

We are a woodworking studio that is passionate about teaching and studying woodworking techniques of all styles and types. The main focus of Hok Tau woodworking is making teaching everyone that woodworking can be a fun and theraputic way to spend time with friends and family by making and sharing crafts.

Here at Hok Tau woodworking, we focus on traditional english woodworking techniques to turn trees into fun and useful objects.
